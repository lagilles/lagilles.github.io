ABOUT ME

Full Stack Web Developer with a passion for technology and improving user experience on the web. I graduated from UNC - Chapel Hill with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Communications and also recently graduated from the UNC Coding Boot Camp. In the Boot Camp, I learned both front end and back end technologies, including, but not limited to, HTML/CSS, Javascript, Node, MySQL, MongoDB, React and more all while working and collaborating with my peers to create interesting and dynamic projects. I originally taught myself HTML and CSS in middle school and continue to follow my passion for web development.

In my spare time, I enjoy running, playing video games, studying Japanese, and snuggling with my French Bulldog, Lulu. I believe that my education, passion and interests give me a unique perspective in the tech industry as a great team player with creative and innovative ideas.
